Motivation

Here is an example of a journal entry.
All the text must be written in pure html, which makes things like formatting and spell-checking more difficult.
But, the end result is exactly what I want it to be, with no exuses to be given to templates or third parties.
The quality is my own to bring.

The journal entries should be edited for clarity and succinctness, but the content should not be filtered. All topics are welcome here in the journal, with only one rule - I must be authentic. As a build on the rule I will also consider the golden principles of speech, before talking one should consider;
- Is this true?
- Is this neccesary?
- Is this kind?
