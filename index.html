What is this place?

This is a place for the articulation of thought and expression through various means, where evidence of industry is laid down for commentary and reflection.

Where weapons of wit and will are mustered to do battle with the forces of self-sabotage, distraction and negativity.

Where craft and process are venerated, with a humble promise to be authentic and vulnerable.

This is a hand-made sanctuary, to calm a busy mind and inspire the celebration of life.

"Welcome ye to my digital home, whereupon you may feast your senses on my work."
